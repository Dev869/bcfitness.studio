Balance and Stability

Virtual Only

This class is perfect for anyone that feels they are losing their balance, have fallen or have a fear of falling. It’s ideal for those that use walkers or canes, and for those with hip issues. This Balance class will focus on rebuilding balance, stability, strength and flexibility by using gentle drills using the Silver Sneaker guidelines.
Liz Cole, a certified Silver Sneakers instructor, has been using these balance drills for her tennis classes, and was so delighted at her students progress she is offering them at BC Fitness and Cafe. The only items you will need will be one sturdy chair and tennis shoes. For more information, call (909) 307-1479 or email info@bikecoach.com.

Indoor Cycling

Virtual Only

This class is designed for those who’d like to use a stationery bike, it a group setting, where there’s a class format instructed with music. This class is favored by those who want a vigorous workout without any pounding on the body. Many people like the feel of riding a bike but fear falling, so these spin bikes are a nice option. The bikes are adjustable for size of each person. The instructor is trained to fit each rider to their bike. They also have knob to adjusting the level of resistance simulating a flat road or a hill. The instructor will guide participants through a workout that incorporates the feeling of different terrain and speed. Sometime there a hills sometimes fast flats. Each class is finished with stretching.

Zumba

Virtual Only

Zumba® Fitness classes take the “work” out of workout, by mixing low-intensity and high-intensity moves for an interval-style, calorie-burning dance fitness party. A total workout, combining all elements of fitness – cardio, muscle conditioning, balance and flexibility, boosted energy and a serious dose of awesome each time you leave class. Zumba® is perfect for everybody and every body. Once the Latin and World rhythms take over, you’ll see why Zumba® Fitness classes are often called exercise in disguise.
